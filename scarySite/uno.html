About Home

One night, Devin and I decided to play the midnight man game. We got our materials ready at about 9:00 pm: papers, white candles, salt, a needle, matches, and a pencil. Time passed as we started formulating our strategy for playing the game. We made sure all the lights in the house were turned off. Soon enough, it was 12:00 am. We each wrote our name on a piece of paper, then we put a drop of our blood on our paper. Before officially starting the game, we each light our candles. Devin helps me synchronize the 22 knocks and I get it just right. I open the door, we blow out our candles, and we close the door. We relight the candles and begin the game.


"Now we're supposed to keep moving or the midnight man will find us or something." Devin says. We leave my room and head to the other side of the house. Devin asks if we're allowed to eat during the game. "I actually don't know," I replied. "Just don't open the fridge because the light might provoke the midnight man and get us disqualified." Devin proceeded to eat, and minutes later, he came back out. I checked my watch by candle light- it's 12:59 and our candles are just fine. I suggested that we head back to the side of the house with my room on it, and we settle on the hallway near the bathroom. Suddenly, my candle goes out.
